Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our leaflet distribution service

How does GPS tracking work?

Our distributors use GPS-enabled devices that record their exact route and timing. You receive a detailed report showing where and when your leaflets were delivered, including map overlays and timestamps.

What's your no competitor policy?

We never deliver competing businesses in the same delivery round. No two takeaways, estate agents, or similar services will be delivered together, ensuring your message gets the attention it deserves.

How quickly can you deliver my campaign?

Typically, smaller campaigns (under 5,000 leaflets) are delivered within 5-7 working days, while larger campaigns may take 1-2 weeks. Delivery times vary based on quantity and current demand, so call us to discuss this further.

What happens if it rains during delivery?

Our distributors use waterproof bags, so delivery will usually go ahead as usual. If weather conditions are severe, we'll reschedule delivery to ensure your leaflets arrive in perfect condition.

Can I choose specific areas for delivery?

Absolutely! We can target specific postcodes, streets, or demographic areas within our coverage zones. Just let us know your requirements during the consultation.

Do you offer design services?

We focus on distribution only, and can't offer any help with designing your leaflets. If you need some recommendations for some local designers, please let us know!
