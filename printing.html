📁 File Requirements

File Format

High-resolution PDF preferred. We also accept AI, EPS, and high-res JPEG files.

Resolution

Minimum 300 DPI for crisp, professional print quality.

Bleed & Margins

3mm bleed around edges, 5mm safety margin for text and important elements.

Color Mode

CMYK color mode for accurate printing. We can convert RGB files if needed.

Printing FAQ

Common questions about our leaflet printing services.

How long does printing take?

Standard printing takes 3-5 working days from approved artwork. Bi-fold leaflets may take an additional 1-2 days due to folding requirements. We coordinate printing with your distribution schedule for seamless service.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Yes, if you'd like the review your leaflet before distribution, we are happy to organise this.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum print run is 1,000 leaflets, which aligns with our minimum distribution quantity for cost-effective campaigns.

Do you offer design services?

Absolutely! We offer complete design services from £50. Our designers can create your leaflet and prepare it for printing in one seamless process, including bi-fold designs.

What makes bi-fold leaflets special?

Bi-fold leaflets offer 4 panels of information in a compact A5 format when folded. They open to reveal the full A4 content, perfect for detailed service information, menus, or premium presentations that need more space.

What if my artwork isn't print-ready?

We can help prepare your files for printing, including adding bleed, adjusting colors, and optimizing resolution.

Can I print without using your distribution?

Our printing service is exclusively for leaflets we distribute to ensure quality control and coordination. Contact us to discuss other arrangements.

How are bi-fold leaflets folded?

Our bi-fold leaflets are precision-folded using professional equipment. They start as A4 size and are folded down to DL size (99×210mm) for easy distribution, creating a professional 4-panel presentation.
