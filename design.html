Design FAQ

Common questions about our leaflet design services.

What do I need to provide?

We need your text content, any images you want included, your logo, brand colors (if any), and a brief description of your goals and target audience.

Can you work with my existing branding?

Absolutely! We'll incorporate your existing brand guidelines, colors, fonts, and style to ensure consistency across all your marketing materials.

What if I don't have professional photos?

We can source high-quality stock images that fit your design, or we can work with photos you provide. We'll advise on image quality requirements.

How many revisions are included?

All packages include up to 3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions can be added for £15 per round if needed.

What file formats do I receive?

You'll receive high-resolution PDFs ready for printing, plus JPEG versions for digital use. Source files (AI/PSD) are available as an add-on.

Can you handle rush jobs?

Yes, we offer expedited design services for urgent projects. Contact us to discuss timeline and rush fees for your specific needs.

💡 Design Tips for Success

Clear Call-to-Action

Make it obvious what you want readers to do - call, visit, or buy.

Keep It Simple

Less is more - focus on one main message per leaflet.

Use Quality Images

Professional photos make a huge difference to perceived quality.

Include Contact Info

Make it easy for people to get in touch with clear contact details.
